Units

PyCAC assumes the use of the following defined molecular units:

  • The unit of time is 10−1210^{-12}10​−12​​ seconds (i.e., picoseconds)
  • The unit of length is 10−1010^{-10}10​−10​​ meters (i.e., Angstroms)
  • The unit of mass is 1.66053904×10−271.66053904\times 10^{-27}1.66053904×10​−27​​ kilograms (i.e., Daltons - unified atomic mass units)
  • The unit of energy is 1.602176565×10−191.602176565\times 10^{-19}1.602176565×10​−19​​ Joules (i.e., eV)
  • The unit of force is 1.602176565×10−91.602176565\times 10^{-9}1.602176565×10​−9​​ Newtons (i.e., eV/Angstrom)
  • The unit of pressure is 10910^910​9​​ Pascales (i.e., GPa)
