EAM potential

As mentioned earlier, the EAM formulation for the potential energy is

E=12∑i∑jj≠iVij(rij)+∑iF(ρ¯i)E = \frac{1}{2}\sum_i\sum_{j \atop j\neq i} V_{ij}(r_{ij}) + \sum_i F(\bar{\rho}_i)E=​2​​1​​∑​i​​∑​​j≠i​j​​​​V​ij​​(r​ij​​)+∑​i​​F(​ρ​¯​​​i​​)

where VVV is the pair potential, FFF is the embedding potential, and ρ¯\bar{\rho}​ρ​¯​​ is the host electron density, i.e.,

ρ¯i=∑jj≠iρij(rij)\bar{\rho}_i = \sum_{j \atop j \neq i} \rho_{ij}(r_{ij})​ρ​¯​​​i​​=∑​​j≠i​j​​​​ρ​ij​​(r​ij​​)

where ρ\rhoρ is the local electron density.

The force is

fk=−∂E∂rk=−12∂∑i∑jj≠iVij(rij)∂rk−∂∑iF(ρ¯i)∂rk\mathbf{f}_k = -\frac{\partial E}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k} = -\frac{1}{2} \frac{\partial \sum_i \sum_{j \atop j \neq i}V_{ij}(r_{ij})}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k}-\frac{\partial \sum_i F(\bar{\rho}_i)}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k}f​k​​=−​∂r​k​​​​∂E​​=−​2​​1​​​∂r​k​​​​∂∑​i​​∑​​j≠i​j​​​​V​ij​​(r​ij​​)​​−​∂r​k​​​​∂∑​i​​F(​ρ​¯​​​i​​)​​

The first term in the force formulation is non-zero only when kkk is either iii or jjj, thus

−12∂∑i∑jj≠iVij(rij)∂rk=−12[∂∑jj≠kVkj(rkj)∂rk+∂∑ik≠iVik(rik)∂rk]=12[∂∑jj≠kVkj(rkj)∂rkjrkjrkj+∂∑ik≠iVik(rik)∂rikrikrik]-\frac{1}{2} \frac{\partial \sum_i \sum_{j \atop j \neq i}V_{ij}(r_{ij})}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k} = -\frac{1}{2} \left[\frac{\partial \sum_{j \atop j \neq k} V_{kj}(r_{kj})}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k}+\frac{\partial \sum_{i \atop k \neq i}V_{ik}(r_{ik})}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k}\right] = \frac{1}{2} \left[\frac{\partial \sum_{j \atop j \neq k} V_{kj}(r_{kj})}{\partial r_{kj}}\frac{\mathbf{r}_{kj}}{r_{kj}}+\frac{\partial \sum_{i \atop k \neq i}V_{ik}(r_{ik})}{\partial r_{ik}}\frac{\mathbf{r}_{ik}}{r_{ik}}\right]−​2​​1​​​∂r​k​​​​∂∑​i​​∑​​j≠i​j​​​​V​ij​​(r​ij​​)​​=−​2​​1​​[​∂r​k​​​​∂∑​​j≠k​j​​​​V​kj​​(r​kj​​)​​+​∂r​k​​​​∂∑​​k≠i​i​​​​V​ik​​(r​ik​​)​​]=​2​​1​​[​∂r​kj​​​​∂∑​​j≠k​j​​​​V​kj​​(r​kj​​)​​​r​kj​​​​r​kj​​​​+​∂r​ik​​​​∂∑​​k≠i​i​​​​V​ik​​(r​ik​​)​​​r​ik​​​​r​ik​​​​]

where VkjV_{kj}V​kj​​ and VikV_{ik}V​ik​​ are the pair potentials for the atomic pairs kjkjkj and ikikik, respectively, and rkj\mathbf{r}_{kj}r​kj​​ is the vector from atom kkk to atom jjj with norm rkjr_{kj}r​kj​​, i.e.,

rkj=rj−rk\mathbf{r}_{kj} = \mathbf{r}_j - \mathbf{r}_kr​kj​​=r​j​​−r​k​​

When the system contains only one type of atoms, VVV for any pair of atoms is the same, so the first term in the force formulation becomes

∑ii≠k∂Vik(rik)∂rkrikrik\sum_{i \atop i \neq k}\frac{\partial V_{ik}(r_{ik})}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k}\frac{\mathbf{r}_{ik}}{r_{ik}}∑​​i≠k​i​​​​​∂r​k​​​​∂V​ik​​(r​ik​​)​​​r​ik​​​​r​ik​​​​

The second term in the force formulation can be written as

−∑i∂F(ρ¯i)∂rk=−∑i∂F(ρ¯i)∂ρ¯i∂ρ¯i∂rk=−∑i∂F(ρ¯i)∂ρ¯i∑jj≠i∂ρij(rij)∂rk-\sum_i\frac{\partial F(\bar{\rho}_i)}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k} = -\sum_i\frac{\partial F(\bar{\rho}_i)}{\partial \bar{\rho}_i}\frac{\partial \bar{\rho}_i}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k} = -\sum_i\frac{\partial F(\bar{\rho}_i)}{\partial \bar{\rho}_i}\sum_{j \atop j \neq i}\frac{\partial \rho_{ij}(r_{ij})}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k}−∑​i​​​∂r​k​​​​∂F(​ρ​¯​​​i​​)​​=−∑​i​​​∂​ρ​¯​​​i​​​​∂F(​ρ​¯​​​i​​)​​​∂r​k​​​​∂​ρ​¯​​​i​​​​=−∑​i​​​∂​ρ​¯​​​i​​​​∂F(​ρ​¯​​​i​​)​​∑​​j≠i​j​​​​​∂r​k​​​​∂ρ​ij​​(r​ij​​)​​

which is non-zero when kkk is either iii or jjj, i.e., the term becomes

−∂F(ρ¯k)∂ρ¯k∑jj≠k∂ρkj(rkj)∂rk−∑ii≠k∂F(ρ¯i)∂ρ¯i∂ρik(rik)∂rk=∂F(ρ¯k)∂ρ¯k∑jj≠k∂ρkj(rkj)∂rkrkjrkj+∑ii≠k∂F(ρ¯i)∂ρ¯i∂ρik(rik)∂rkrikrik-\frac{\partial F(\bar{\rho}_k)}{\partial \bar{\rho}_k}\sum_{j \atop j \neq k}\frac{\partial \rho_{kj}(r_{kj})}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k}-\sum_{i \atop i \neq k}\frac{\partial F(\bar{\rho}_i)}{\partial \bar{\rho}_i}\frac{\partial \rho_{ik}(r_{ik})}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k} = \frac{\partial F(\bar{\rho}_k)}{\partial \bar{\rho}_k}\sum_{j \atop j \neq k}\frac{\partial \rho_{kj}(r_{kj})}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k}\frac{\mathbf{r}_{kj}}{r_{kj}}+\sum_{i \atop i \neq k}\frac{\partial F(\bar{\rho}_i)}{\partial \bar{\rho}_i}\frac{\partial \rho_{ik}(r_{ik})}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k}\frac{\mathbf{r}_{ik}}{r_{ik}}−​∂​ρ​¯​​​k​​​​∂F(​ρ​¯​​​k​​)​​∑​​j≠k​j​​​​​∂r​k​​​​∂ρ​kj​​(r​kj​​)​​−∑​​i≠k​i​​​​​∂​ρ​¯​​​i​​​​∂F(​ρ​¯​​​i​​)​​​∂r​k​​​​∂ρ​ik​​(r​ik​​)​​=​∂​ρ​¯​​​k​​​​∂F(​ρ​¯​​​k​​)​​∑​​j≠k​j​​​​​∂r​k​​​​∂ρ​kj​​(r​kj​​)​​​r​kj​​​​r​kj​​​​+∑​​i≠k​i​​​​​∂​ρ​¯​​​i​​​​∂F(​ρ​¯​​​i​​)​​​∂r​k​​​​∂ρ​ik​​(r​ik​​)​​​r​ik​​​​r​ik​​​​

Again, when the system contains only one type of atoms, ρ\rhoρ for any pair of atoms is the same, so the second term in the force formulation becomes

∑ii≠k(∂F(ρ¯k)∂ρ¯k+∂F(ρ¯i)∂ρ¯i)∂ρik(rik)∂rkrikrik\sum_{i \atop i \neq k}\left(\frac{\partial F(\bar{\rho}_k)}{\partial \bar{\rho}_k}+\frac{\partial F(\bar{\rho}_i)}{\partial \bar{\rho}_i}\right)\frac{\partial \rho_{ik}(r_{ik})}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k}\frac{\mathbf{r}_{ik}}{r_{ik}}∑​​i≠k​i​​​​(​∂​ρ​¯​​​k​​​​∂F(​ρ​¯​​​k​​)​​+​∂​ρ​¯​​​i​​​​∂F(​ρ​¯​​​i​​)​​)​∂r​k​​​​∂ρ​ik​​(r​ik​​)​​​r​ik​​​​r​ik​​​​

In all, a general EAM force formulation is

fk=12[∂∑jj≠kVkj(rkj)∂rkjrkjrkj+∂∑ik≠iVik(rik)∂rikrikrik]+∑ii≠k(∂F(ρ¯k)∂ρ¯k+∂F(ρ¯i)∂ρ¯i)∂ρik(rik)∂rkrikrik\mathbf{f}_k = \frac{1}{2} \left[\frac{\partial \sum_{j \atop j \neq k} V_{kj}(r_{kj})}{\partial r_{kj}}\frac{\mathbf{r}_{kj}}{r_{kj}}+\frac{\partial \sum_{i \atop k \neq i}V_{ik}(r_{ik})}{\partial r_{ik}}\frac{\mathbf{r}_{ik}}{r_{ik}}\right] + \sum_{i \atop i \neq k}\left(\frac{\partial F(\bar{\rho}_k)}{\partial \bar{\rho}_k}+\frac{\partial F(\bar{\rho}_i)}{\partial \bar{\rho}_i}\right)\frac{\partial \rho_{ik}(r_{ik})}{\partial \mathbf{r}_k}\frac{\mathbf{r}_{ik}}{r_{ik}}f​k​​=​2​​1​​[​∂r​kj​​​​∂∑​​j≠k​j​​​​V​kj​​(r​kj​​)​​​r​kj​​​​r​kj​​​​+​∂r​ik​​​​∂∑​​k≠i​i​​​​V​ik​​(r​ik​​)​​​r​ik​​​​r​ik​​​​]+∑​​i≠k​i​​​​(​∂​ρ​¯​​​k​​​​∂F(​ρ​¯​​​k​​)​​+​∂​ρ​¯​​​i​​​​∂F(​ρ​¯​​​i​​)​​)​∂r​k​​​​∂ρ​ik​​(r​ik​​)​​​r​ik​​​​r​ik​​​​
